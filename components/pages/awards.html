Awards

The New York Art Directors Club — Gold. For United Technologies ad campaign

The Westchester Art Directors Club — Numerous awards for design recognition

The PMA Reggie Awards — A solid list of wins including one for Hispanic Marketing

Design of the Times — For outstanding in-store campaigns and merchandising inititatives

The OMAs — Six successive wins for Holiday Displays, as well as a slew of Gold, Silver and Bronze Indian Chiefs for a host of programs

The Adam’s Award — Best-in-class beverage merchandising Kraft

Vendor Service Award — For Global Employer Branding Campaign

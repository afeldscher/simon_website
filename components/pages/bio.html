Simon Mark Haddad

Simon co-conceived and developed Thr3e Marketing, Eastwest Marketing Group’s wholly-owned subsidiary focusing on "Thr3tailing," a discipline that transcends shopper marketing and focuses on three core constituents: the shopper, retailer and brand — helping to activate purchase decisions. At Thr3e, Simon worked with agency teams and their brands to develop integrated initiatives and creative activation that included: merchandising, customer programs and partnerships, competitive analyses as well as aligning brands with technology trends.

Prior to founding Thr3e, Simon worked with Eastwest Marketing Group where he helped build and support consumer growth for companies such as Labatt USA, Kraft Foods, Bayer, TAG Heuer and Reckitt Benckiser through creative implementation efforts.

Before joining Eastwest Marketing Group, Simon spent several years at Marketing Corporation of America leading a creative team in developing contemporary creative solutions for an account roster that included ABC television, Coca-Cola, Grady's American Grill, LIPTON Tea, Frito-Lay, IBM ThinkPad and Topps Candy & Gum. Moreover, His creative background focused on international and domestic advertising and promotion for a wide range of categories including communication, entertainment and packaged goods.

Simon's passions include playing his guitar, skiing, cycling, automobile racing, motorcycling, scuba diving, soccer and spending time with his wife and son.
